The creation of this program is an idea of a friend (I hope he is my friend) who often solves riddles with letters. This program generates all possible combinations of certain letters without repeating them. For example, ABC will generate:
ACB
BAC
BCA
CBA
CAB
BC
CB
C
How to use it? Just write the letters in the textarea bellow and click on "generate permutations" button.
